" Traditional Algerian Chorba Recipes **"

chorba is a classic Algerian soup, often made with lamb or chicken, vegetables, and unique ingredients like frike (cracked wheat), offering hearty, aromatic flavors that reflect Algerian culinary heritage.

Ingredients

  • 100 g meat ( lamb, beef, or chicken, small pieces)
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 tomato grated or blended
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 handful chickpeas ( cooked or from a can )
  • 2 tbsp frik (crushed wheat or fine bulgur)
  • 2 tbsp oil or smen (clarified butter)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon ( optional)
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsiey or coriander
  • 1 liter water

Preparation

  1. in a pot heat oil, add onion, meat, spices, and fry for 5 minutes
  2. add tomato and tomato paste stir well
  3. pour in water add chickpear, and let simmer 25-30 minute
  4. add frik ( or bulgur ) and cook 15 more minutes
  5. sprinkle fresh parsley or coriander before serving

👍 Seve hot with lemon 🍋 wedges and bread 🥖
